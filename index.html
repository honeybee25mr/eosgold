World views

Before the wounds of the war were gone, the surviving humans started the war to take over the hegemony of the recovered continent. However, this war has become fiercer for different reasons than before. This is because artifacts with strange powers have been found all over the world. These relics were a mixture of magic and science, and those who hold them in their hands had wealth, honor, and great power.


This world has entered a new era of war. In this war and chaos, you have to choose which side to take. Will you strive for balance and harmony? Would you use the power of relics to take control of wealth and honor?

Character

EOS RED Occupation

Warrior

  • A warrior who uses inborn strength and health to stand on the front line, Trusts only their own weapon and strength, takes on confrontation, and is Good at close combat.

Archer

  • The archer is one who can freely control arrows. They can remotely shoot fatal arrows to kill the enemies, Is the expert of distant combat.

Rogue

  • An assassin who has high Evade and Attack ability, and can attack enemies swiftly from the dark. The weapon used is the Double Wield Dagger.

Sorceress

  • She is a wizard who handles various magic with her natural magic. Burns enemies with powerful flames and heals allies. Uses magic power to fight.

Paladin

  • Hybrid Class with physical and magical attacks. Use a shield and blunt weapon to charge and punish its opponents. She specialized in both support and frontline.

Warlock

  • A battle wizard using a long-range attack using a magic sword and a curse using a wands. A class that shines in group battles, such as attracting targets or reducing healing power.
